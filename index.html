CercaMe — Account Deletion

At CercaMe, you are always in control of your data. You can request the deletion of your account at any time directly from the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the CercaMe app
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  3. Confirm your request

Deletion timeline

Day 0 – Request received

  • Your account is immediately disabled
  • You can no longer log in

Day 7 – Data purge

  • Photos, albums, events, stores, visits, and personal information are removed
  • Your profile is anonymized

Day 30 – Final deletion

  • All remaining data is permanently erased

Legal retention

If the account is involved in reports, investigations, or legal requirements, deletion may be temporarily delayed until the process is resolved.

What data is deleted

  • Profile information
  • Photos and albums
  • Messages and chat history
  • Events and stores created
  • Activity logs and visits
